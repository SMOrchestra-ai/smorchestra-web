What this is

Not consulting. Not an agency retainer. An embedded transformation.

Big-4 consultancies leave your board with a deck and a slide showing how AI could work at your company. We leave your enterprise with an AI-Native Organization actually running — the same 5-plugin OS our smaller clients install, but adapted to your vertical, deployed at enterprise scale, and embedded inside your existing structure for 6 to 18 months until your internal team can run it solo.

A board mandate for "AI-native" is not a slide-ware problem. It's an operational problem. The only way to solve it is by installing an org that operates AI-native — and training your people inside it.

Typical engagement profile: a KSA or UAE enterprise with 100+ employees, a sitting CxO with explicit AI transformation mandate, and internal digital/IT teams who cannot deliver at the pace the market demands. Board reporting cycle 6-12 months. Strong preference for founder-led practitioner delivery over Big-4 consultancy theater.

Who this is for

Enterprise profile we typically engage.

  • Company size: 100 to 1,000+ employees. MENA-headquartered or with MENA regional HQ (KSA + UAE).
  • Revenue: AED 50M - 500M+ / year. Public, PIF-portfolio, or well-capitalized private.
  • Sector: Fintech (STCPay-adjacent, regional banks digitizing), logistics and supply chain, healthcare, edtech, government-adjacent sovereign portfolio companies, regional HQs of global firms expanding into KSA.
  • Mandate: Board-level AI-native transformation directive with a 6-12 month reporting cycle. Saudization alignment, Vision 2030 KPIs, or regulatory compliance (ZATCA, PDPL) often drive the mandate.

  • Not for: Pre-PMF startups (see Tier 3). Enterprises wanting a deck and PowerPoint (we don't do theater). Companies without CEO sponsorship.

How it works

Engagement structure.

  • Private Briefing (30 min): Mamoun personally. No deck. We understand your mandate, your sponsor dynamic, the specific business lines targeted for transformation, and your internal team readiness.
  • NDA + Deeper Scoping: Mutual NDA. Follow-up calls with relevant stakeholders (CxO sponsor, CFO, CTO, legal). Shape the engagement envelope.
  • Strategic Partnership Workshop (1-2 days): Paid workshop (AED 25,000-50,000) to map the transformation blueprint. Deliverables: plugin architecture adapted to your vertical · embedded team profile · governance model · phased timeline · metric definitions.
  • Engagement Letter: Bespoke engagement letter. Pricing reflects scope: typically AED 500,000+ initial engagement. Retainer tail AED 50,000-150,000/month during the embedded phase. Equity or revenue-share is on the table for the right strategic deal.
  • Embedded Delivery (6-18 months): Senior team on the ground. Weekly syncs with sponsor. Monthly board updates. Quarterly governance reviews. Custom plugin development running in parallel.
  • Hand-off + Optional Ongoing: Internal team takes over primary operations. Optional ongoing Advisory Circle engagement (AED 25,000+ per quarter) for strategic oversight post-hand-off.
