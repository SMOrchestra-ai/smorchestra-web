FAQ

The questions every B2B founder asks on call one.

Who is AI Revenue OS actually for?

B2B founders and revenue leaders operating in MENA, past product-market fit, scaling past the point where manual ops and hand-cranked GTM can keep up. If you're pre-PMF, we'll tell you. If you're Enterprise-sized and need a 40-person RevOps team, we're not your match either. Sweet spot: $500k–$10M ARR, 5–40 people, MENA-based or expanding into MENA.

How is this different from hiring a RevOps consultant?

A consultant leaves you with a deck. AI Revenue OS leaves you with a running system. We build it, operate it with you for 90 days, then transfer the keys. You own the code, the data, the workflows, and the AI agents outright. No lock-in. No retainer. No rebuild if you change consultants next year.

What does "transferred" actually mean?

On day 91, we hand over a documented system running on your infrastructure, under your accounts, in your name. Your team is trained. Your SOPs are written. Your monthly run cost is typically under $200. If we vanished tomorrow, nothing would break.

Why MENA-specific?

Because WhatsApp is the business spine here, buyers close in Gulf dialect not MSA, and the RevOps talent pool is structurally thinner than in the US or EU. A localized Western playbook quietly fails. AI Revenue OS is engineered for the market that actually exists — not the one Salesforce writes marketing about.

How long until it's running?

Build phase is 8 weeks. Operate phase is 12 weeks. Transfer is on week 21. You see outputs in week 2 and measurable pipeline impact by week 6.

Can I just buy the Ops layer or just the GTM layer?

Yes — we also install Ops-only and GTM-only. But AI Revenue OS is the fused version because, in practice, Ops without GTM becomes a well-organized pipeline of nothing, and GTM without Ops becomes a firehose into a CRM nobody updates. The leverage shows up when both layers are running.

What happens if my team can't run it after transfer?

They can. We trained them for 12 weeks on live data, not whiteboards. But if you want us to stay on as occasional operators (think: a couple of hours a week at founder-advisor rates, not a retainer), we're happy to. It's an option — not a lock-in.
