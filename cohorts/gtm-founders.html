FAQ

Questions every applicant asks before committing.

Is this for B2B or B2C?

Both, but the cohort is curated so we don't mix too much. Current cohorts lean B2B-heavy; we open a B2C-only variant when the waitlist depth justifies it.

Language?

Bilingual. Sessions are in English but cohort discussion, WhatsApp channel, and async work can be Arabic or English. We write the localization module assuming you'll publish in both.

Do I need a product in market?

Ideally yes — at least an MVP. If you're pre-product, we'll tell you on the fit call. Sometimes we'll route pre-product founders to another group; sometimes we'll say "come back in 90 days."

Is there a certificate?

No. You walk out with a running engine, not a PDF. The certificate is the customers you close in the next 90 days.

Investment?

Scoped per cohort. We keep it accessible for founders because the cohort is the bet we're making on the next wave of MENA operators — not a revenue line we're optimizing.
