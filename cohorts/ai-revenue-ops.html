Fit Check

Who this is — and isn't — for.

We run small cohorts on purpose. 12 seats max. If the fit is wrong, we'll tell you on the application call and point you to AI Revenue OS (done-for-you) or nothing.

Built for you if…

  • You're a B2B founder or revenue leader, past PMF, 5–40 people
  • You have a pipeline that exists but doesn't scale
  • You'd rather build it yourself than hand it to an agency
  • You can commit 8 hours/week for 8 weeks
  • You want to own the system, not rent capability

Skip if…

  • You're pre-PMF — a system won't fix positioning
  • You want someone to build it for you (that's AI Revenue OS)

FAQ

What applicants ask before they commit.

How is this different from a course?

A course gives you videos. This gives you an engine. Every week you ship — no slides, no homework for homework's sake. By week 8 your system is running on your data. If you haven't shipped, you haven't passed.

Is this Arabic or English?

English for Phase 1. Arabic cohort will launch in Phase 2 (late 2026). The content engine you build can publish in both — localization is covered as a week-7 module regardless of cohort language.

Who's in the room?

Mamoun + one senior operator every live session. The cohort itself is 12 B2B founders and revenue leaders screened on the application call. You build alongside your peer cohort, not in isolation.

What's the investment?

Scoped on the application call. We price cohorts to be the fraction of AI Revenue OS that makes sense for founders doing the install themselves — but we don't list it publicly because fit matters more than price.

What if I fall behind?

The cohort moves. You get the recordings + office hours to catch up. If you miss more than 2 weeks live, we'll suggest rolling into the next cohort — no penalty.
